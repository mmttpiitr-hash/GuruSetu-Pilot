# Welcome to GuruSetu Pilot

Welcome to the **GuruSetu Pilot program** — a content testing phase designed to bring you early access to emerging learning materials in faculty development.

## About the Program

GuruSetu Pilot is a collaborative initiative where faculty like you play a crucial role in shaping the future of educational content. Your feedback and engagement help us create better, more relevant courses for a national rollout.

### Quick Start

- **New to the program?** [Learn more about what to expect](/faq/)
- **Have questions about ViBe?** [Check our FAQs](/faq/#part-2-about-the-vibe-platform)
- **Ready to get started?** Visit [gurusetu.org](https://gurusetu.org) to register

## What You'll Find Here

- **Interactive Learning Videos** - Access curated video content tailored for educators
- **Feedback & Discussion** - Share your insights and help shape the program
- **Support Resources** - Find answers to common questions and troubleshooting tips

## Key Information

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Time Commitment** | ~15-45 minutes per video |
| **Pace** | Self-paced, no fixed schedule |
| **Flexibility** | Choose what interests you |
| **Impact** | Your feedback directly influences the final courses |

## Get Involved

Your participation is valuable and straightforward:

1. **Browse** the available videos on the ViBe platform
2. **Watch** content that interests you (or watch at 2x speed!)
3. **Share** your feedback through the feedback forms

Even watching **one video per week** is considered meaningful participation.
