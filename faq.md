# GuruSetu Pilot — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

> This document provides answers to common questions about the GuruSetu Pilot and the ViBe platform. It is intended to help participants navigate the platform, understand expectations, and resolve common issues.

## Part 1: About the GuruSetu Pilot

### 1. What is the GuruSetu Pilot?

The GuruSetu Pilot is a **content testing phase** where early versions of learning materials are shared with faculty. These materials will later be developed into formal courses and modules for a national rollout. Your role is to review the content and provide feedback that will directly shape these final courses.

---

### 2. What am I expected to do?

Your participation involves three simple steps:

1. Browse the available videos on the platform
2. Watch any video that interests you
3. Submit the feedback form linked to that video

> There is no fixed order or sequence — you can choose what you engage with.

---

### 3. Do I have to complete all the videos?

No. This is not a traditional course. There is:

- No requirement to complete all videos
- No fixed "completion" point

You are free to engage with as many videos as you find relevant.

---

### 4. How much time does this take?

| Activity | Estimated Time |
|---|---|
| Each video | ~15 to 45 minutes |
| Watching at 2x speed | Reduces time significantly |
| Feedback form | ~1–2 minutes |

> Even watching **one video per week** is considered meaningful participation.

---

### 5. Why is the feedback form locked initially?

The feedback form unlocks **only after you watch the video**. This ensures that feedback is:

- Based on actual engagement
- Thoughtful and relevant

---

### 6. What kind of feedback are you looking for?

We value **meaningful and honest feedback**, such as:

- What worked well
- What was unclear or could be improved
- How relevant the content is to your teaching context

> This is not about completing the form quickly — your feedback will directly influence the final course design.

---

### 7. What does "progress" mean in this pilot?

Your progress reflects:

- Videos watched
- Feedback forms submitted

Since new content will be added periodically, progress is **dynamic and continuously evolving**.

---

### 8. What does it mean to be "active" on the platform?

To be considered active, you should:

- Watch at least **one video per week**, OR
- Log in periodically and engage with content

This helps ensure that participants in the pilot are actively contributing.

---

### 9. Why was I removed from the platform?

To keep the pilot cohort engaged and manageable, participants with **no activity for over two weeks** may be temporarily removed. However:

- You can **rejoin anytime** using the pilot link
- Please note that previous progress may not always be retained

---

### 10. Will I receive a certificate?

This is a pilot phase, not a formal course, so certificates are not being issued at this time. However:

- Your participation will be **formally acknowledged**
- Based on the final number of videos in the pilot, a threshold will be defined
- Participants who meet this threshold (by watching videos and submitting feedback) will receive an **acknowledgement of participation**

> Further details will be shared soon.

---

### 11. Will new content be added?

Yes. New videos will be added **periodically**, and you can choose to engage with them at your convenience.

---

### 12. Why should I participate in the pilot?

By participating, you are:

- Helping shape a **national-level faculty development initiative**
- Contributing to content that will impact **thousands of educators**
- Getting **early access** to emerging ideas and practices

## Part 2: About the ViBe Platform

### 1. What is the ViBe platform?

ViBe is a **Learning Management System (LMS)** designed to deliver course content in a focused learning environment. It includes minor proctoring features that track participant attention during the course to encourage engagement while reading or learning the content.

---

### 2. How do I register for the GuruSetu Pilot on ViBe?

Follow these steps to register:

1. Visit [gurusetu.org](https://gurusetu.org)
2. Click on **Register for GuruSetu Program**
3. You will be redirected to the ViBe platform login page
4. Log in using **Continue with Google**
5. Once you reach your dashboard, go to **Recommended Courses**
6. Select **GuruSetu Pilot**
7. Click **Register Now**
8. Fill out the registration form with your details
9. Enter the course dashboard

> **Note:** Before accessing the course, you will see a declaration form requesting permission for **camera access** and **microphone access**. These permissions are required by the software to create a focused learning environment and verify participant presence.

---

### 3. Can I use a mobile phone or tablet to access ViBe?

**No.** The ViBe platform currently supports **only desktop or laptop devices**. Mobile phones and tablets are not supported.

---

### 4. I'm facing video issues on ViBe (video stuck, looping, or skipping). What should I do?

Please try these troubleshooting steps in order:

1. **Refresh the page** and try again multiple times
2. **Log out and log back in**
3. Try using a **different browser**
4. **Clear your browser cache** and browsing data, then log in again
5. **Inspect browser errors:**
   - Right-click on the page → click **Inspect**
   - Open the **Network** or **Console** tab
   - Try playing the video and check for visible errors

> If the issue still persists, share your query along with a **screen recording** at the support email. Our team will review and get back to you.

---

### 5. What are penalty scores on ViBe, and how do they affect my progress?

Penalty scores are generated when **anomalies are detected** during your activity on the platform, such as irregular behavior while:

- Watching video lessons
- Attempting quizzes

If your penalty score becomes high, you may be required to **rewatch the video lesson**.

> **Important:** Penalty scores currently **do not affect your performance evaluation or final scoring**. Their primary purpose is to ensure proper engagement with the learning content.
