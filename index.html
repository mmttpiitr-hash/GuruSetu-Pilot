GuruSetu Pilot FAQs

Support & Frequently Asked Questions

Part 1: About the GuruSetu Pilot

1. What is the GuruSetu Pilot?

The GuruSetu Pilot is a content testing phase where early learning materials are shared with faculty for review and feedback.

2. What am I expected to do?
  1. Browse videos on the platform
  2. Watch any video of interest
  3. Submit the linked feedback form
3. Do I have to complete all videos?

No. There is no requirement to complete all videos or follow a fixed sequence.

4. How much time does this take?
  • Each video: 15–45 mins
  • Feedback form: 1–2 mins
  • Watching at 2x reduces time
5. Why is the feedback form locked?

The feedback form unlocks only after watching the video to ensure meaningful feedback.

6. What feedback is expected?

Share honest feedback on clarity, usefulness, improvements, and relevance.

7. What does progress mean?

Progress reflects videos watched and feedback forms submitted.

8. What does active participation mean?

Watch at least one video per week or log in regularly to engage with content.

9. Why was I removed?

Participants inactive for over two weeks may be removed but can rejoin anytime.

10. Will I receive a certificate?

No certificate currently. Active participants will receive acknowledgement of participation.

11. Will new content be added?

Yes, new videos will be added periodically.

12. Why should I participate?

You help shape a national-level faculty development initiative and get early access to content.

Part 2: About ViBe Platform

1. What is ViBe?

ViBe is an LMS platform with minor proctoring and engagement tracking features.

2. How do I register?
  1. Visit gurusetu.org
  2. Click Register for GuruSetu Program
  3. Login with Google
  4. Select GuruSetu Pilot
  5. Click Register Now
3. Can I use mobile or tablet?

No. Only desktop or laptop devices are supported.

4. Facing video issues?
  • Refresh page
  • Re-login
  • Try another browser
  • Clear cache
5. What are penalty scores?

Penalty scores are generated for detected anomalies and may require rewatching videos.

6. When to use Flag option?

Use Flag only for content issues. For technical issues, email: mmttpiitr@gmail.com
